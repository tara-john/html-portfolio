Top 3 Movies

Get Out

Iconic Jordan Peele filme with layers of symbolism and dissecting issues such as race and class

Parasite

A korean masterpiece that analyses class structures in Korean society through the lens of one family that infiltrates the household of a wealthier family

Everything Everywhere All At Once

A feast for the senses, a movie about the passage of time explored in a other-wordly multi-verse format with an outstanding cast
